Jujutsu Kaisen Image Gallery

Jujutsu Kaisen features a diverse cast of characters who fight against Curses. The anime follows high school student Yuji Itadori, who becomes entangled in Jujutsu after swallowing a cursed object. He is mentored by Satoru Gojo and bonds with classmates Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki. They confront Cursed Spirits like Mahito and Ryomen Sukuna, and other key characters include Maki Zenin, Toge Inumaki, and Panda
